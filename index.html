Skybridge 737 Vote to Replace Condo Board

Overview

This is an independent page made by Bobby Heidkamp, an owner at Skybridge, for informational purposes. My goal is to increase vote turnout.

Management sent out a notice for three meetings and proxies on Oct 25th:

  1. Mon, Nov 4 - Vote to remove current board members
  2. Wed, Nov 6 - Vote to elect new board members if vacancies arise from the removal vote
  3. Wed, Nov 6 - The Current board set this date to pass The Special Assessment
    • If the current board remains after the removal vote, the special assessment will be passed without further exploration & due diligence on Nov 6th.
    • If a new board has a majority with new members, they can reject & reevaluate the special assessment and proceed with proper due diligence in a timely manner, ensuring emergency work continues without interruption.

Voting By Proxy

  • The Proxies provided with the notices will allow you to cast your vote regardless of your ability to be present at these meetings.
  • To make the process smoother, electronic forms were sent via PandaDoc for e-signing.
    • Please check your Spam/Junk folders if you don’t see them in your mailbox. The email is from docs@email.pandadoc.net.
    • It will also assist in keeping track of the progress of the removal vote. Life happens, so let’s all take this step to make sure we can count on every vote.

THE PROXIES ARE IMPORTANT TO MEET THE HIGH BAR OF 2/3 (~67%) OWNERSHIP VOTE REQUIRED

Additional Voting Options

Voting can also be completed by:

  • Printing, completing, scanning, and emailing back to the condo CPA at vote@condocpa.com
  • Printing, completing, and placing in the Ballot Box in the lobby
  • Participating directly in the meeting
