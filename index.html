Vote to Replace Condo Board

Overview

Management sent out a notice for three meetings and proxies on Oct 25th:

  1. Mon, Nov 4 - Vote to remove current board members
  2. Wed, Nov 6 - Vote to elect new board members if vacancies arise from the removal vote
  3. Wed, Nov 6 - If the current board remains, they will pass the Special Assessment without further due diligence

If a new board has majority representation, they can reject, reevaluate, and proceed with proper due diligence, ensuring emergency work continues without interruption.

Proxies

  • The proxies provided allow you to cast your vote if you cannot attend the meetings.
  • Electronic forms were sent via PandaDoc for e-signing. Please check Spam/Junk folders if not received, or contact docs@email.pandadoc.net for assistance.
  • Proxies are critical to achieving the 2/3 (67%) vote threshold.

Action Steps

  • Please complete and sign proxies via PandaDoc ASAP to streamline the process.
